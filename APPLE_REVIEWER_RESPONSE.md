RESPONSE TO GUIDELINE 2.1 - LOCATING IN-APP PURCHASES

The in-app purchases are available and fully functional. Instructions to locate them:

Method 1 - Through Settings (Most Direct):
1. Launch app and complete onboarding (swipe 3 intro screens, tap "Get Started")
2. Tap hamburger menu (top-left)
3. Select "Settings"
4. Scroll to "Account" section
5. Tap "Manage Subscription"
6. Paywall displays both subscription options: Premium Yearly ($35.99/year) and Premium Monthly ($5.99/month)

Method 2 - Through Prayer Journal:
1. Launch app and complete onboarding
2. Tap "My Prayers" from Quick Actions
3. Tap "+ Add Prayer" button
4. Fill in prayer request
5. Tap "Get AI Guidance"
6. After 15 messages, paywall appears

Method 3 - Through Scripture Chat:
1. Launch app and complete onboarding
2. Tap "Scripture Chat" from Quick Actions
3. Start conversation with AI assistant
4. After 15 messages, paywall appears
