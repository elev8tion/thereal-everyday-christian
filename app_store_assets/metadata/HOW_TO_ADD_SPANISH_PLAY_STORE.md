# How to Add Spanish Language to Google Play Store

## Step-by-Step Guide

### 1. Access Google Play Console
1. Go to https://play.google.com/console
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Select **"Everyday Christian"** app

### 2. Navigate to Store Listing
1. In the left sidebar, click **"Grow"**
2. Click **"Store presence"**
3. Click **"Main store listing"**

### 3. Add Spanish Language
1. Look for **"Add language"** button (usually in the top right corner)
2. Click **"Add language"**
3. From the dropdown, select:
   - **"Spanish (Spain)" - es-ES** OR
   - **"Spanish (Latin America)" - es-419** (recommended for broader reach)
4. Click **"Add"**

### 4. Fill in Spanish Content
Copy the content from `SPANISH_PLAY_STORE_LISTING.md` file:

#### App Details Tab
- **App name:** Everyday Christian (keep same, or localize if desired)
- **Short description:** (Copy from SPANISH_PLAY_STORE_LISTING.md)
- **Full description:** (Copy from SPANISH_PLAY_STORE_LISTING.md)

#### Graphics Tab
Upload the same graphics or Spanish-specific ones:
- **App icon:** `app_store_assets/icons/spanish/playstore_icon_512.png`
- **Feature graphic:** `app_store_assets/screenshots/android/Feature_graphic-2.png`
- **Screenshots:** Upload at least 2 (from `app_store_assets/screenshots/android/`)
  - Phone screenshots (required)
  - Tablet screenshots (optional)

### 5. Store Settings
1. Click **"Store settings"** in the left sidebar
2. Under **"App details"**:
   - **Category:** Lifestyle
   - **Tags:** Add Spanish keywords from SPANISH_PLAY_STORE_LISTING.md

### 6. What's New Section
1. Go to **"Release"** ‚Üí **"Production"**
2. For current release, add Spanish translation of release notes
3. Copy "Notas de Lanzamiento" from SPANISH_PLAY_STORE_LISTING.md

### 7. Review and Save
1. Click **"Review"** at the top
2. Check for any errors or warnings
3. Click **"Save"** (bottom right)
4. Click **"Submit update"** if ready to publish

## Important Notes

### Language Priority
- Google Play will automatically show Spanish listing to users with Spanish device language
- English will be shown as fallback to non-Spanish users

### Which Spanish?
- **es-ES (Spain):** European Spanish
- **es-419 (Latin America):** Recommended - covers Mexico, Central & South America
- **Both:** You can add both if you want to customize for each region

### Required vs Optional Fields

**Required:**
- App name
- Short description
- Full description
- At least 2 phone screenshots
- Feature graphic
- App icon

**Optional:**
- Promotional video
- Tablet screenshots
- 7" tablet screenshots
- 10" tablet screenshots

### Screenshots
You can either:
1. Use the **same English screenshots** (app switches to Spanish automatically)
2. Take **new Spanish screenshots** with Spanish device language
3. Add **text overlays** in Spanish to existing screenshots

### Testing Before Publishing
1. After saving, don't submit immediately
2. Use **"View on Google Play"** button to preview
3. Change your device language to Spanish
4. Check how the listing appears
5. Make any necessary corrections
